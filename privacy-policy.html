Privacy Policy for Task Up

Last Updated: December 25, 2025

Overview

Task Up ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains how our to-do list application handles your information.

Information Collection and Usage

Data Storage

Task Up stores all your task data locally on your device only. We do not:

  • Collect any personal information
  • Transmit data to external servers
  • Access your contacts, location, or other device data
  • Use analytics or tracking services
  • Share data with third parties

What We Store Locally

The app stores the following information on your device using local storage:

  • Task titles and descriptions
  • Task due dates
  • Task priority levels
  • Task completion status

This data is stored exclusively on your device and is never transmitted over the internet.

Data Security

All task data is stored locally using your device's secure storage system (AsyncStorage). The data remains on your device and is protected by your device's security measures.

Data Deletion

You have complete control over your data:

  • Delete individual tasks at any time within the app
  • Uninstalling the app will permanently remove all stored task data from your device
  • We cannot recover your data once deleted as we do not store it on any servers

Children's Privacy

Task Up does not knowingly collect any information from children. The app can be used by anyone and does not require any personal information.

Third-Party Services

Task Up does not integrate with any third-party services, analytics tools, or advertising networks.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the app's listing and documentation with an updated "Last Updated" date.

Your Rights

Since all data is stored locally on your device:

  • You have complete ownership of your data
  • You can export or delete your data at any time by managing tasks within the app
  • No data is shared with us or any third parties

Consent
